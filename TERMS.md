## 2. The Service

DiVA is a Discord bot powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. It can:

- Reply to chat messages when mentioned, replied to, or DM'd
- Set, list, snooze, reschedule, and cancel reminders (one-shot or recurring)
- Send a daily "secretary" digest of upcoming reminders
- Run a small set of natural-language administrative actions in your server (kicking, channel management, etc.) on behalf of authorized administrators
- Adapt a per-server "personality" based on recent conversations
- Track AI usage cost on a per-server credit balance

Features and behavior may change over time. Material changes are announced in your server's notices channel.
